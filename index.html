About

Hello! I am Alan Guerrero a Front-End Developer / Full Stack Web Developer, originally native to Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic!

I am a Computer Science graduate from NJIT (New Jersey Institute of Technology), and I am currently aiming to finish my master's degree; however, I am pretty much self taught in the art of Web Development. I started working as a Front-End Web Developer in 2012 and ever since have grown a strong interest for Interactive, Front-End and Full Stack Web Development.

My passion is in the ever evolving web, how to make it better, more efficient and more visually engaging to the user. I have worked for numerous companies both full time and as a contractor; however, I also enjoy being involved in open source projects, learning new web technologies and somehow introduce my new found knowledge into my endeavors.
